ABOUT THE COMPANY

Key features of our company

Compliant Equity Tokenization

ChainEquity enables SMEs to issue blockchain-based equity tokens that align with UK FCA and EU MiCA regulations, bridging traditional finance with Web3, without compromising legal integrity.

No IPO, No Ownership Loss

Unlike traditional fundraising or IPO routes, ChainEquity lets founders raise capital without giving up control, preserving decision-making power while attracting investors globally.

Global Investor Access

Tokenized equity offers round-the-clock liquidity and access to cross-border investors via decentralized infrastructure, removing the geographic and time-based limits of legacy markets.

SME-Focused Design & Onboarding

The platform is built specifically for non-technical SME owners, with automated legal workflows, intuitive onboarding, and integrated investor tools, eliminating the complexity of blockchain adoption.
